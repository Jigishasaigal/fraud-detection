V14:
Represents deviation from normal transaction behavior.
Extreme negative values are strongly associated with fraudulent transactions,
especially in card-not-present fraud.

V12:
Captures latent anomaly patterns in transaction flow.
Large negative values often indicate abnormal usage inconsistent with past behavior.

V4:
Encodes non-linear transaction characteristics.
Unusual values may reflect suspicious transaction timing or behavior patterns.

Amount:
Transaction amount alone is not a reliable fraud indicator.
Both low-value and high-value transactions can be fraudulent depending on context.

Time:
Represents seconds elapsed between transactions.
Time has limited standalone predictive power but can amplify risk when combined with other features.
