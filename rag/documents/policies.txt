Policy 1:
Transactions showing abnormal latent behavior (e.g., V14, V12 anomalies)
should be reviewed or blocked even if transaction amount is low.

Policy 2:
Low-value transactions with strong anomaly indicators may signal card testing fraud
and require preventive blocking.

Policy 3:
Transactions classified as low risk with no anomaly indicators
can be safely approved without manual review.

Policy 4:
Decision thresholds and risk categorization are optimized offline
based on financial cost and operational constraints.
